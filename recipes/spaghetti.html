Spaghetti

Description

The following is a recipe for making spaghetti, an Italian recipe famous all over the world.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 2 pounds lean ground beef
  • 1 onion, minced
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 2 (10.75 ounce) cans condensed tomato soup
  • 2 1/2 cups water

Steps:

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to boil.
  2. Add pasta and cook for 8-10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
  3. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook ground beef with onion until meat is brown.
  4. Drain.
  5. Return meat mixture to pan with tomato paste, tomato soupd and water.
  6. Cook, stirring, until heated through and thickened, 5-10 minutes.
  7. Serve over cooked pasta.
